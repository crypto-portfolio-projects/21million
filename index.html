How to Buy 21M?

1

Create a Wallet

Download and set up a wallet that supports Binance Smart Chain, like MetaMask or Trust Wallet. Securely store your recovery phrase and password to protect your digital assets.

2

Get Some BNB

Purchase Binance Coin (BNB) from a trusted exchange and transfer it to your wallet. BNB is needed for transaction fees and to swap for 21M tokens.

3

Go to PancakeSwap

Visit PancakeSwap and connect your wallet. Ensure you are on the Binance Smart Chain network to enable smooth trading.

4

Swap BNB for 21M

Select the swap option on PancakeSwap and enter the amount of BNB you wish to trade. Adjust settings like slippage if necessary, then confirm the swap to receive your 21M tokens.
